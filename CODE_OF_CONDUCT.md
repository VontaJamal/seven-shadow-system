# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration across all discussions and reviews.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while staying direct about risks.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks or deliberate hostility.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to: conduct@rinshari.com

Maintainers may remove comments, close PRs/issues, or suspend participation for repeated violations.
